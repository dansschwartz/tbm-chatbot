# AI-Powered Chatbot for Your Organization

## What It Is

An intelligent chatbot that lives on your website and answers visitor questions instantly — 24/7, using your own content. When it can't answer, it seamlessly connects visitors with your team.

Think of it as a tireless front-desk assistant that knows everything on your website and never takes a day off.

## How It Works

1. **We pull all the content from your website** — programs, FAQs, policies, schedules, pricing, staff contacts, everything
2. **The chatbot learns it** — using AI to understand your content deeply, not just keyword matching
3. **Visitors get instant answers** — grounded in YOUR information, with source links
4. **When it can't help, it connects to your team** — contact form, email routing, or human handoff

## What Visitors See

A small chat bubble in the bottom-right corner of your website. Click it, and:

- 💬 **Instant answers** to common questions (registration, schedules, costs, policies)
- ⚡ **Quick reply buttons** for the most popular topics
- 📧 **Contact form** to reach your team when needed
- ⭐ **Satisfaction rating** so you know how well it's performing
- 🔗 **Source links** so visitors can verify answers on your site

## What Admins Get

- 📊 **Analytics dashboard** — see what visitors ask about most, satisfaction scores, busiest times
- ❓ **Unanswered questions log** — know exactly where your content has gaps
- 🧠 **Guidance rules** — tell the bot "always recommend financial aid" or "direct refund questions to this email"
- 📋 **Contact requests** — every human handoff captured with visitor info
- 🔗 **Webhook integration** — real-time notifications to your existing systems
- 💰 **Usage tracking** — full transparency on costs and volume
