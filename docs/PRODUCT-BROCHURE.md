# TBM AI Assistant
### Intelligent Chat Support for Not-for-Profit Organizations

---

## Your website visitors have questions. Answer them instantly — 24/7.

TBM AI Assistant is an intelligent chatbot that lives on your website, answers visitor questions in real time, and seamlessly connects people with your team when needed. Built specifically for not-for-profit organizations, it's affordable, easy to set up, and gives you the tools enterprise companies pay thousands for.

**One line of code. Your entire website's knowledge. Instant answers.**

---

## How It Works

```
1. We pull your website content → programs, FAQs, policies, schedules, contacts
2. AI learns it deeply → not keyword matching — actual understanding
3. Visitors get instant answers → grounded in YOUR information, with source links
4. When it can't help → seamless handoff to your team via email/contact form
5. You get insights → see what people ask, where gaps are, how the bot performs
```

---

## Features

### 🤖 AI-Powered Answers
- Answers questions using **your own content** — never makes things up
- Cites sources so visitors can verify on your site
- Suggests follow-up questions after each answer
- Detects language and responds in English or Spanish
- Learns from your entire website — FAQs, policies, schedules, pricing, staff contacts

### 💬 Smart Chat Widget
- Beautiful, customizable chat bubble on your website
- **Your brand colors**, logo, name, and avatar
- Pre-chat form to collect visitor name & email before chatting
- Quick-reply buttons for the most common questions
- Proactive greeting — "Need help with registration?" pops up after a few seconds
- Works perfectly on mobile and desktop
- Chat history persists across page navigation
- Download chat transcript

### 🧑‍💼 Human Handoff & Support
- Built-in contact form — visitors can email your team directly from the chat
- Auto-detects frustration and offers to connect with a person
- Configurable business hours with custom "away" message
- All contact requests logged and accessible in admin dashboard
- Email notifications when someone reaches out

### 📊 Admin Dashboard & Analytics
- **Real-time conversation monitor** — see what's happening on your site right now
- Total conversations, messages, resolution rate
- **CSAT ratings** — visitors rate their experience (1-5 stars)
- **Top question topics** — automatically tagged (registration, financial aid, tryouts...)
- **Unanswered questions log** — shows exactly where your content has gaps
- **Thumbs up/down feedback** on every bot response
- **Response time tracking** — prove the bot is faster than email
- **Smart insights** — auto-generated recommendations based on your data
- Export conversations as CSV or JSON
- Full-text search across all conversations

### 🔧 Admin Control
- Edit the bot's personality, tone, and rules without code
- **Guidance rules** — "always recommend financial aid," "never discuss competitors"
- **Banned words filter** — keep responses appropriate
- **Scheduled messages** — "Spring registration is now open!" for date ranges
- **A/B testing** — test different welcome messages, see which performs better
- **API key rotation** for security
- **Knowledge base article browser** — visitors can browse your content without chatting

### 🔗 Platform Integration
- **REST API** — manage everything programmatically
- **Webhooks** — get real-time notifications (new contacts, negative feedback, escalations)
- **Bulk content upload** — push content from your systems via API
- **Auto-crawler** — enter a URL, we pull the content automatically
- **PDF upload support** — upload documents directly
- **Multi-tenant** — manage multiple organizations from one platform
- **WhatsApp integration** — same AI, different channel (via Twilio)
