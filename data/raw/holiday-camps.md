HOLIDAY CAMPS 
 
  
 DCSC offers Holiday Camps throughout the school year on holidays and school closure days, providing a convenient option for families when school is not in session. Whether it’s a one, two, or three-day break, these camps give parents peace of mind knowing their children are engaged in a structured, positive program while they work or manage other responsibilities.Holiday Camps are offered each fall, winter, and spring season, with registration posted seasonally below. Camps are available as single-day or multi-day registrations, with half-day and full-day options to fit family schedules.Each camp blends fun with soccer skill development under the guidance of DC Soccer Club’s professional coaching staff. Designed for players of all abilities, participants are grouped by age and level to ensure the right mix of challenge and support. The curriculum emphasizes specific technical skills and decision-making, reinforced through engaging activities and small-sided games that bring out tactical awareness and creativity on the field. ELIGIBILITY:  K - 6th Grade, age group may change depending on specific camp listed 
 PROGRAM DETAILS: Each holiday camp day is 9am-3pm; week-long camp over Spring Break is available as half day (9am-12pm) or full day (9am-3pm)Holiday camps are typically offered in a NW and NE tentative location, with specific location confirmed after registration opens due to permit process.Open to all levels, players grouped by age and competitive levelPlayers should bring a ball, refillable water bottle, snacks and lunch. The should wear shin guards and dress appropriately for the weather.
