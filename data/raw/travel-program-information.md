 DC Soccer Club’s Travel Program is designed for players and families ready to make a stronger commitment to soccer and player development. With greater commitment comes greater reward: players who dedicate themselves to regular training and competition see the most growth in their skills, confidence, and connection with their teammates. Our program offers three levels —  
Select, Travel, and Academy  
— so each player can develop at the pace and intensity best suited for them. Commitment to the Travel Program is driven by competitive player development through the tryout process, not placement by friend request or practice location.Practice schedules and field locations are determined by the permits the club secures each season. 
Families are expected to support and adapt to the team schedule, understanding that many factors are considered to ensure all players and teams have the opportunity to train and compete successfully. 
﻿While the level of competition and time commitment may vary across Select, Travel, and Academy, all teams share the same expectation: consistent attendance at practices and games with a focus on player growth and development. 
At DC Soccer Club, we recognize that the financial commitment of Travel soccer can be significant, and we encourage families to  
apply for financial aid  
to understand what support may be available before accepting a team invitation.  
We also encourage families to follow DC Soccer Club on our social media so they share a greater connection to our club, and receive the latest news and events--including tryouts--about our travel program.
