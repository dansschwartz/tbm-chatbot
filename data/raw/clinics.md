DC Soccer Club offers training clinics for supplemental development, giving players and adults the chance to sharpen skills and gain confidence outside of their regular team environment. Clinics provide focused attention, extra repetitions and professional coaching to help players build technical ability, decision-making, and enjoyment of the game.

Throughout the year, we offer a range of clinic opportunities that may focus on technical skills like receiving, dribbling, or finishing, or provide specialized training such as goalkeeper development. Clinics are open to recreational players who want to build a stronger foundation, travel players looking for more targeted growth, and even adults who want to learn or build skills, scrimmage, and enjoy the social side of soccer.

We also host flexible formats in the off season such as drop-in sessions, where players of all levels come together for organized free play with no coaching. At other times, we offer specialty clinics that target areas like shooting to improve confidence around the goal area, or sports performance to develop the athlete and improve confidence on the soccer field by training speed, agility, coordination, balance and strength.

## Rec Goalkeeper Clinic

## Adult Clinic

![](https://sspark.genspark.ai/cfimages?u1=6lzS%2FEHBZaQkUet8WxwpgyCmDMiocodTeFZXq%2FCXbl1Ae4KJLSQkAsPwzTdAQJ%2Fih175DyWffsRhAz0ctXrbsXUsFExxNKDrQaqorNji6Efdjbz3YGt%2F%2Bf2YKHoOEw%3D%3D&u2=y%2BrX4J6HvO1j%2B54X&width=1024)

The DCSC Adult Clinic is offered each season throughout the year for ages 18+. Designed for both newcomers learning the fundamentals and lifelong players looking to sharpen their skills, the six-week program combines skill development, small-sided games, and plenty of fun. Led by professional coaches, it’s a supportive, social way to stay active, build confidence, and enjoy the game with others.

[Learn More](https://www.dcsoccerclub.org/adult-clinic)
