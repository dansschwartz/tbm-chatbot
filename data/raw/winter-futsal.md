 DCSC FUTSAL RULES 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 Winter Futsal League - Frequently Asked Questions 
 
 
 
 
 Can we form our own team?Yes. If you have a collection of kids who want to play together, each will register individually but must request the same coach or reference the same team name to request during registration.Will there be field time available for practices?No. Our Winter Futsal League is set up as games only on Saturdays -- HELD OUTDOORS--and there are no practices scheduled, or practice slots available for teams. What are the time slots for Futsal games? When does play begin?Saturdays 9am-5pm. Games will start during the fourth weekend in January.What uniforms are teams required to wear? The only requirement is that  players on the same team should wear the same color. Each team may choose to wear their rec uniforms or create new shirts, or a coach may bring pinnies -- just as long as players wear the same color. The only exception is that GOALKEEPERS must wear a different collor shirt or use a pinnie of another color then the field players. Should players bring a soccer ball? Do they need to buy a futsal ball?DC Soccer Club will provide game balls on game days. The refs will have futsal balls for use in games. No one needs to buy a futsal ball unless they wish to practice outside of the game. Players can warm up on game days with regular soccer balls.Where do you find the game schedule?By mid-January, registered players can access their team's game schedule on their account in PlayMetrics.
