CAMPS 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 DCSC offers a variety of camp experiences throughout the year designed to meet the needs of players at every stage of their development. Holiday Camps & Traditional Summer Camps When school is closed, families often look for meaningful ways to keep their children active, engaged, and supported throughout the day. Our Holiday Camps and week-long Summer Camps provide exactly that. Offered in both traditional and advanced formats, these camps are available for full-day or half-day participation and welcome players of all levels, unless otherwise noted. Camps balance skill development with fun games and activities, allowing players to build confidence, enjoy time with friends, and stay active in a safe environment.Advanced Development with Elite Training CampFor our competitive Travel program players, DCSC offers the Elite Training Camp during select weeks in the summer and over spring break. This camp is designed as a high-intensity, immersive experience that mirrors the demands of the travel environment. Led by professional coaches, the Elite Camp helps players sharpen technical skills, tactical awareness, and overall fitness so they return to their teams stronger and prepared for the season ahead. 
 
 
 
  Why Families Choose DCSC Camps 
 
  
 Beyond soccer, our camps provide a valuable solution for families seeking full-day options when school is not in session. Players not only develop their skills on the field but also benefit from structured routines, healthy activity, and meaningful social interaction with peers. Parents appreciate knowing their children are in a positive, professional environment where they can grow as athletes and individuals while staying active and engaged all day long. 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 Holiday Camps 
 
  
 Holiday Camps  
are scheduled on holidays or school closure days, with both half-day and full-day registration options available as well as options to register for single or multiple days for multiple holiday dates. Led by DC Soccer Club’s professional coaching staff, these camps blend engaging activities with soccer skill development, ensuring players stay active, learn, and have fun when school is out.

 Summer Camps 
 
  
 Our traditional 
Summer Camps  are open to all levels and are held weekly during the summer for full-day or part-day registration. Combining  fun with soccer development. Each day focuses on a different topic, taught through technical activities and games to bring out the skill development through repetitions and tactical application.

 Elite Camps 
 
  
 Offered for several weeks over the summer and during spring break, the 
Elite Camp is designed exclusively for Travel players aged U9-U19 and provides a high-level training experience to push competitive development during seasonal breaks. The week-long summer camp consists of 2.5 hours of training each to maximize intensity.
