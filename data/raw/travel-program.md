TRAVEL PROGRAM 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 DC SOCCER CLUB ACCEPTED INTO MLS NEXT ACADEMY DIVISION AND GIRLS ACADEMY (GA) ASPIRE BEGINNING 2026–2027 SEASON Register for DCSC Academy ID Sessions 
 
 
 
 
 DC Soccer Club is committed to providing the highest standard soccer experience for players in the Washington, DC area and offers a travel program structure for players to be placed at various competitive levels and a pathway for players to develop.Our club is proud to serve players in all 8 wards of the District, as well as players from Maryland and Virginia, to form more than 70 travel teams competing at various competitive levels in the  
MLS NEXT Academy Division (beginning 2026-2027 Season), 
Girls Academy (GA) ASPIRE (beginning 2026-2027 Season), CPSL, EDP, NCSL and MDSL.  
The club's philosophy is based on a complete player-development model. This model heavily emphasizes individual player development, while progressing their understanding of team tactics and the performance environment as they age. The Travel Program's structure allows for identification and development opportunities throughout each age group. With several teams in each age group, we offer the staff and leagues aligned with each team's competitive level to nurture development. Players are placed where they have an optimal balance between success and challenge.Please read below about our competitive levels in the Travel pathway that include Academy, Travel and Select
