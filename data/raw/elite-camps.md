ELITE CAMPS 
 
  
 Designed exclusively for our 
travel players aged 9-19, the DCSC Elite Camp provides a high-level training experience to take your game to the next level over the summer. Under the guidance of Director of Soccer Matt Arrington and experienced guest coaches, players will focus on advanced technical ball work, team tactics, positional training and match simulations. This camp challenges athletes to apply their skills in competitive, game-like scenarios while refining their understanding of the game. Players will leave with an enhanced understanding of the game, positional components, and principles of play. It’s the ultimate environment for dedicated players to sharpen their craft and excel on the field. ELIGIBILITY:  Travel players U9 - U19 
 PROGRAM DETAILS: Week-long camp registration offered multiple weeks in the summer and during spring break week9:30am-12pm  Monday - Friday during camp weekOne camp location only each week; For summer camps, specific field announced in mid-May due to the DC permit allocation timeline and processPlayers should bring a ball, refillable water bottle and snacks. Players should wear shin guards and dress appropriately for the weather.
