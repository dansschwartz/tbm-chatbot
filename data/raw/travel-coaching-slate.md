 Below you will find our 
Travel  Program coaching slate, organized by team and age group, along with key staff contacts who support each level of the program. Our coaching staff is supported by a structured leadership team to ensure a high-quality, consistent experience for all players.In addition, we have outlined our 
communication pathway, designed to ensure questions and concerns are addressed efficiently by the appropriate staff member.For current players and families, we ask that all communication follows the order outlined below:Step 1: Team Coach Please begin by contacting your 
team coach, who is best positioned to address questions related to training, games, and the team environment.Step 2: 
 Age Group Director If your question or concern is not resolved, please reach out to your 
Age Group Director, who oversees your player’s age group and can provide additional support.Step 3: 
 Program Leadership (by team type) If further assistance is needed, please contact the appropriate program lead based on your team: Select Teams: Travel Director – Amin HassAcademy Teams: Academy Manager – Neill OuskaGirls Teams: Girls Director – Ann GermainBoys Teams: Boys Director – Anthony Ugorji 
Step 4: 
 Technical Director For continued escalation, please contact 
Technical Director Edu de Souza.Step 5: 
 Director of Soccer If your concern is still not resolved, please contact 
Director of Soccer Matt Arrington.We appreciate your cooperation in following this structure, which helps ensure clear communication, appropriate support, and a positive experience for all players and families.
