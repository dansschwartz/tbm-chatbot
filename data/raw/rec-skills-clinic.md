Rec Skills Clinic 
 
  
 The DC Soccer Club Recreation Skills Clinic is a six-week program designed to help players build confidence and improve their technical ability in a supportive, high-energy setting. Led by our professional coaching staff, the clinic emphasizes ball control, agility, precision, and creativity—core elements that make a difference in every player’s game.Open to players of all levels, the clinic focuses on individual skill development through engaging drills and small-sided activities that maximize touches on the ball. Whether your child is just starting out or looking to sharpen specific techniques, this program offers an excellent supplement to recreational league play.By concentrating on movement, mastery of the ball, and confidence in decision-making, the Rec Skills Clinic helps players strengthen their foundation, preparing them for more competitive opportunities while still keeping the fun and excitement of the game at the center. ELIGIBILITY:  1st - 8th grade 
 PROGRAM DETAILS: 6 weekly sessions; 1 hour eachOffered in fall and spring seasonsGeared towards recreational players of all levelsPlayers should bring a ball and water
