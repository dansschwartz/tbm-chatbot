SUMMER CAMPS 
 
  
 DC Soccer Club Summer Camps provide an exciting and dynamic experience for players of all abilities. Designed to keep kids active and engaged during the summer months, our camps combine skill development, fun activities, and plenty of play. Players participate in technical skill activities , small-sided games and fun competitions that enhance their technical, tactical, and physical growth—all while enjoying the energy and community of camp.Led by DCSC’s professional coaching staff, Summer Camps offer a supportive and high-energy environment where players can improve their soccer skills, build confidence, make new friends, and create lasting memories. ELIGIBILITY:  Kindergarten - 8th Grade 
 PROGRAM DETAILS: Week-long summer camp registration offered June - AugustPart-day (9am-12pm) or full-day (9am-3pm) registrationOffered in both NW and NE locations. Specific field locations announced in mid-May due to the DC permit allocation timeline and processOpen to all levels, players grouped by age and competitive levelPlayers should bring a ball, refillable water bottle, snacks and full-day campers should bring lunch. Players should wear shin guards and dress appropriately for the weather. 
 COST:  
Full-day camp week (9am-3pm): $350 + processing feesHalf-day camp week (9am-12pm): $200 + processing feesFinancial Aid available to applyCONTACT: Program Administrator
