SCHOOL PROGRAMMING

 Bringing Soccer to Schools Across the District
DC Soccer Club
partners with schools across Washington, D.C. to bring high-quality soccer programming directly to students in their own school communities. Through collaboration with school administrators and teachers, DCSC provides customized, developmentally appropriate programs that promote fitness, teamwork, confidence, and fun.Our goal is simple — to make soccer accessible to all children by meeting them where they are, and by working hand-in-hand with schools to create enriching physical activity opportunities during or after the school day. Program Overview
Each partnership begins with a conversation about your school’s needs. Together, we determine: The
length of the program
(typically 6–8 weeks, but flexible based on your schedule)Days and times
that fit your school calendar and facilitiesCurriculum design
tailored to your student age groups and goals
Programs are typically held on school grounds, outdoors on school athletic fields or indoors in a school gymnasium.
If space is unavailable on-site, DCSC can assist in
securing a nearby DPR or DCPS field
to host the sessions.Our experienced coaching staff leads all sessions and provides the
soccer balls, equipment, and training curriculum. We can also handle
participant registration
directly through the DCSC platform, streamlining administration for the school. Curriculum & Customization
DCSC offers programming for: Tots & Preschool Ages
– Play-based activities focused on movement, balance, and funElementary School Students
– Skill-building in dribbling, passing, teamwork, and game playMiddle School Students
– Emphasis on strategy, competition, and leadershipHigh School Students
– Advanced sessions to improve fitness, technical skill, and game awareness
Each program is designed to fit your students’ developmental stage and desired outcomes. Examples include: An
8-week, once-a-week afterschool programA
6-week, twice-a-week enrichment blockA
short-term intensive program designed to fit unique school schedules or calendar needsNo matter the format, DCSC ensures every session fosters teamwork, confidence, and joy through soccer.
