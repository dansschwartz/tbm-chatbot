For more information about our travel program and tryout process: Travel ProgramTravel Tryout FAQs
AGE GROUP CHANGE FOR 2026-2027 SEASONPlease note US Soccer has moved to the
age-group cutoff of August 1-July 31
to determine eligibility for each age group beginning in the 2026-2027 season, and our travel tryouts in spring 2026 will be set accordingly. Age Group Change Information
REGISTER FOR TRYOUTSAll players must register to attend tryouts.
This includes current DCSC Select players, DCSC Travel players,  and any other players interested in trying out for our Travel Program. Choose TRAVEL in the eligible age group if presented with option of Travel or SelectRegister for Tryouts
ATTENDING TRYOUTS Players should plan on attending both sessions if possible for maximum evaluationPlayers should arrive at least 30 minutes prior to tryout start time to check in at the tent and receive their tryout number (paper bib) to pin on their shorts for identificationPlayers should make sure to check in for SELECT TRYOUTS since Travel Level tryouts will also be taking placePlayers should bring water and be dressed to train weather appropriate and wear cleats, shinguards, etc.
FINANCIAL AIDFamilies interested in
applying for financial aid
for the 2026-2027 travel season
must wait until April 20 to apply. We encourage families to apply at that time to understand what aid they may receive before accepting an invitation to join a travel team for next year as financial aid can not be applied retroactively. Financial Aid Information
