#### A Celebration of Soccer, Community, and Purpose

Each spring, **DC Soccer Club** hosts its annual **Fútbol Fiesta Fundraiser**, bringing together families, coaches, and supporters for an evening of celebration and impact. The event—typically held in **late spring (June)**—is the club’s **most important fundraiser of the year**, directly supporting the programs, players, and coaches that make DCSC a thriving part of the Washington, D.C. community.

Fútbol Fiesta raises funds that help expand player scholarships, enhance coach education, and strengthen the programs that shape every level of our club—from the youngest Recreational players to our most competitive Travel teams.

####
Why the Fútbol Fiesta Matters

The fundraiser ensures that DCSC can continue to grow in a way that reflects our mission and core values of **Intention, Inclusion, Collaboration, and Community**. Every ticket purchased, sponsorship made, or auction item bid on directly supports:

* **Program Enhancement & Expansion:** Creating new opportunities like specialized clinics, cultural enrichment programs, and international soccer experiences.
* **Player Scholarships & Accessibility:** Providing financial assistance so that more children from all eight wards of the District can play and grow within our programs.
* **Coach & Player Development:** Supporting advanced coaching education, licensing, and player pathways that lead from youth to collegiate and international levels.

#### An Evening to Remember

The Fútbol Fiesta isn’t just a fundraiser—it’s a celebration of everything that makes DC Soccer Club special. It’s a night to connect with others who share the same passion for the game, raise a glass to another successful season, and help ensure that every player, regardless of background, has a chance to be part of our community.
