 DC Soccer Club is the District of Columbia's largest youth sports program with more than 3,500 players participating in its Recreational Program each fall and spring. The success of our Rec program is simple: We provide a place for players of all abilities to play in an environment focused on fun that is neighborhood based. Built through the service of volunteer coaches and commissioners, the program is low-cost and maintains a lighter commitment level for families to enjoy the foundation of organized soccer. We also offer financial aid for families that qualify.DCSC provides a coed soccer league for players in Pre-K - Grade 12, and a girls-only league for players in Grades 1 - 12.  Nearly half of all recreational players are girls, making DC Soccer Club the largest youth sports program for girls in the city.
For more information specific to the Pre-K age group within our Rec League registration, please go to our
Pre-K Plus Program.DC Soccer Club's recreational program is offered in both fall and spring, with practices held once per week localized in neighborhoods and eight league games across DC on Saturdays. Players are placed on teams requested or based on school to ensure a convenient location and social engagement. The fall season begins after Labor Day and extends into the early part of November. The spring season begins in early April and runs through mid-June.  ELIGIBILITY:  Grades Pre-K - Grades 12
