Developing comfort, confidence, and precision with the ball is one of the most important steps in a player’s soccer journey. The Ball Mastery Clinic focuses on improving foot skills—the foundation of every training session and a key to success at any level. By strengthening a player’s touch, they can unlock better control, decision-making, and enjoyment of the game.

Training is designed around the individual needs of each player, with a focus on:

* **Technical Skill Development:** Coaches teach players how to move with and receive the ball so they can handle game situations with greater confidence.
* **Creative Freedom:** Sessions encourage players to try new skills, take risks, and apply techniques in a supportive environment that gradually increases pressure.
* **Small-Sided Games:** Players practice applying their technical skills in realistic, game-like scenarios that also build awareness of team play.
* **Repetitions:** Frequent touches on the ball help players improve proficiency and consistency, creating a strong foundation for growth.
* **ELIGIBILITY****:**  U8-U16 players

* **PROGRAM DETAILS:**
* 6 weekly sessions; 1 hour each
* Geared towards travel/select level players, open to all
* Players should bring a ball and water, and dress appropriately for the weather
