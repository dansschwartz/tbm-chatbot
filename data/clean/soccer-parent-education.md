SOCCER PARENT EDUCATION

 At DC Soccer Club, we believe soccer should be inspiring — both for players and those who support them. That’s why we’ve partnered with Soccer Parenting Association and secured a
free membership to the
Soccer Parent Resource Center for every parent and coach in our club.What You’ll Find in the Resource CenterInside
SoccerParentResourceCenter.com, you’ll discover a rich library of tools designed to empower you in your role as a supporter, guide, and mentor: Courses & On-Demand Video LessonsMonthly Live Webinars with expert panelistsArticles & Interviews with coaches, sport scientists, and soccer parenting prosParent & Coach Community Forums for peer supportGuidance, tips, and frameworks—all aimed at helping your child love the game and get the most out of their soccer experience

Why We’ve Partnered with the Soccer Parenting AssociationWe’re proud to be a Club Member of the Soccer Parenting Association, because we share a vision: when parents, coaches, clubs, and players are aligned, youth soccer becomes more positive, enriching, and developmental.The mission of the Soccer Parenting Association is to
Inspire Players by Empowering Parents
— and that mission aligns perfectly with ours. We hope, as you explore the Resource Center, you’ll find both information and community that make your journey more confident, connected, and effective.Soccer Parenting was founded by
Skye Eddy, a youth coach, former collegiate player, and coach educator. She created this platform after wrestling with how to support her own children in sports — balancing encouragement, pressure, and personal growth.Today, the
Resource Center reflects years of collaboration with experts across sports psychology, coaching, development, and parenting — all distilled into practical, applicable content for families like ours.
