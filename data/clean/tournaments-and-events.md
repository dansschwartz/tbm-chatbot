  All-Star Cup

  November 8, 2025 - 2ND GRADE-6TH GRADE - COED AND GIRLS REC TEAMS - 5V5 TEAMS

 DC Soccer Club’s annual tournament celebrating the all-stars in our Recreational League. This event is by invitation only to selected players of age groups, forming teams coached by our rec coaches in a friendly and fun day of competition.

  The District Cup

  December 6-7, 2025 - U9-U14 Boys and girls travel teams

 DC Soccer Club hosts its annual end-of-season travel tournament the first weekend of December at premium turf fields around Washington, DC, with tournament headquarters stationed at the Fields at RFK.
