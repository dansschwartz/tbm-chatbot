THE DISTRICT CUP

 District Cup Tournament Cancellation NoticeDue to unsafe field conditions across DC—including the closure of RFK Stadium fields because of snow and ice—the 2025 District Cup Tournament has been cancelled. Player safety is our top priority, and the current conditions do not allow us to move forward safely. Our Tournament Director will be in direct contact with all registered teams regarding next steps, including information on refunds and any potential alternative options. Thank you for your understanding and support.

 DC Soccer Club is excited to host the annual District Cup Tournament, taking place Dec. 6-7, 2025, for boys and girls travel teams U9-U14 in Washington, D.C. This tournament is a great opportunity for teams to compete in a competitive travel tournament at the end of their fall season to showcase their growth and development.
In addition to an exciting weekend of games, players will have the chance to explore the nation’s capital with family members and teammates during their downtime. Popular spots to see might include the White House, the Smithsonian, the Washington Monument, or any of DC's other great attractions.The Tournament will be held exclusively on premium turf fields throughout the Nation's Capital, with tournament headquarters held at the Fields at RFK.
